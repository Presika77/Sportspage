Nepal Women's National Football Team

Nickname: Gorkhali Chelis

The Nepal Women's National Football Team began its journey in the mid-1980s, debuting at the 1986 AFC Women's Championship. Despite early challenges, including heavy defeats, the team showed resilience and passion. After a long hiatus, they returned in 2010 and have since become a dominant force in South Asia, reaching the finals of the SAFF Women's Championship six times. Their motto, "Football for Change," reflects their mission to empower women through sport and inspire future generations across Nepal.

Governing Body: All Nepal Football Association (ANFA)

Coach: Kumar Thapa

Top Scorer: Sabitra Bhandari (53 goals)

Team Motto: Football for Change

Home Ground: Dasharath Stadium
